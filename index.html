Meet Sean

After fighting cancer

Sean started More Than 4 Athletics with Ohio State and LA Chargers star - Joey Bosa. M4 Raises money and awareness for childhood cancer through sports. With the help of sportswear giant Adidas, Sean developed branding worn by athletes in the NFL, CFL, NCAA, PLL, and more. Notable initiatives include the NFL My Cause My Cleats event and the Premier Lacrosse League Championship Series. Sean graduated with distinction from the Samford University Brock School of Business. Outside of philanthropy, Sean was a team liaison for the Huadenoeuanee Lacrosse teams at the 2022 World Games, has worked as Director of Operations for Samford Women’s Soccer (Division 1), and founded the Southern Men’s Lacrosse League. While in college, Sean worked as an intern for the Kansas City Chiefs and Fox Sports/United States Football League.
